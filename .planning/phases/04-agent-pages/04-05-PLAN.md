**Design spec:**

The card is a Link to `/agents/{slug}`. Structure:

1. **Large portrait photo** (aspect-ratio 3:4) fills the card. Use `next/image` with `fill` and `object-cover`. Image has a subtle `grayscale(20%)` filter at rest that transitions to `grayscale(0)` on hover (CSS transition 500ms).

2. **Bottom info bar** (always visible): A gradient overlay (`bg-gradient-to-t from-black/80 via-black/40 to-transparent`) at the bottom of the photo area. Contains:
   - Agent's full name in `font-heading text-xl font-bold text-white`
   - Title in `text-sm text-gold tracking-wider uppercase font-label`
   - A thin gold line separator (`h-px w-12 bg-gold/60`) between name and title

3. **Hover overlay**: On group-hover, a semi-transparent overlay (`bg-near-black/70 backdrop-blur-sm`) slides up from the bottom (use `translate-y-full group-hover:translate-y-0 transition-transform duration-500 ease-out`). This overlay contains:
   - Agent's shortBio in `text-sm text-white/80 line-clamp-3`
   - Stats row: "{propertiesSold} Sold | {totalVolume} Volume" in `text-xs text-gold`
   - Contact info: phone and email in `text-sm text-white/70`
   - A "View Profile" CTA with ArrowRight icon in gold, aligned bottom-right
   - The email should use `onClick` with `stopPropagation` + `e.preventDefault()` to open mailto without navigating (same pattern as current implementation, per decision AGENT-CARD-EMAIL-01)

4. **Card border**: At rest, `border border-border/20 rounded-xl overflow-hidden`. On hover: `hover:border-gold/40` with `hover:shadow-[0_0_30px_rgba(212,175,55,0.1)]` for a subtle gold glow.

5. **Scale effect**: `transition-transform duration-500 group-hover:scale-[1.02]` on the outer container.

Keep the component as `"use client"` since it uses onClick handlers. Import: `Image` from next/image, `Link` from next/link, `ArrowRight` from lucide-react, `Agent` type from @/data/agents.

Do NOT use any card-tilt class (remove it). The premium hover overlay replaces the old card-tilt interaction.
