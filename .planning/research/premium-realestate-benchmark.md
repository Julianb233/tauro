# Premium Real Estate Website Benchmark
## Comprehensive Feature & Element Audit

**Date:** 2026-03-19
**Brokerages Researched:** Compass, Sotheby's International Realty, Douglas Elliman, The Agency, Corcoran, Coldwell Banker Global Luxury, Christie's International Real Estate, Engel & Volkers

---

## 1. HERO / ABOVE THE FOLD

### Background & Visual Treatment
1. **Full-bleed cinematic video hero** — auto-playing, muted drone footage of skylines, coastlines, or landmark properties (Coldwell Banker, The Agency, Sotheby's)
2. **High-resolution photography hero** — single aspirational property image, professionally shot (Compass, Christie's)
3. **Slideshow/carousel hero** — rotating featured properties or lifestyle images with smooth crossfade transitions (Elliman, Engel & Volkers)
4. **Dark overlay gradient** on hero media for text legibility (all premium sites)
5. **Parallax scrolling effect** on hero background image/video (The Agency, Corcoran)
6. **Scroll indicator icon** (animated chevron or mouse icon) prompting users to scroll down (Coldwell Banker)

### Search Functionality
7. **Prominent search bar** centered in hero section, accepting: city, neighborhood, address, school, ZIP, agent name, MLS number (Compass)
8. **Tabbed search modes** — Buy / Rent / Sell tabs above search input (Compass, Elliman)
9. **Autocomplete/predictive search** with dropdown suggestions as user types (all premium sites)
10. **Location-based search** accepting city, state, province, country, postal code (Engel & Volkers, Christie's)
11. **AI-powered visual search** — upload photos of home features you love, AI finds matching listings (Elliman "Inspirations" feature)

### CTAs & Messaging
12. **Aspirational headline** — "Where Do You Want to Go?" (Elliman), "Home to the Extraordinary" (Coldwell Banker)
13. **Primary CTA button** — "Start Your Search" / "Find Your Home" prominently styled (Elliman, Compass)
14. **Secondary CTA** — "Sell Your Home" / "Get a Home Valuation" as persistent seller-lead prompt (Compass, Engel & Volkers)
15. **Tagline/value proposition** — concise luxury positioning statement ("We are leaders in luxury properties")

### Navigation Style
16. **Transparent header overlay** on hero, becoming opaque/solid on scroll (Compass, Coldwell Banker, Christie's, Engel & Volkers)
17. **Logo left-aligned** with clean wordmark/monogram (all sites)
18. **Mega-menu dropdowns** with structured categories: Buy > Countries/Cities/Lifestyles (Christie's, Sotheby's)
19. **Utility navigation** — currency selector, metric/imperial toggle, sign in/register (Coldwell Banker, Engel & Volkers)
20. **Hamburger menu** on mobile expanding to full-screen overlay with smooth slide-in animation (all sites)
21. **Sticky header** maintaining brand presence during scroll, often with reduced height (Compass, Coldwell Banker)

## 6. DESIGN & UX ELEMENTS

### Typography
163. **Custom serif fonts for headlines** — conveying tradition, authority, luxury (Prata, Playfair Display at The Agency)
164. **Clean sans-serif for body text** — high readability (Lato, Nunito Sans, Open Sans, Brandon Grotesque)
165. **Oversized statement headlines** functioning as design elements, not just text (Christie's, Coldwell Banker)
166. **Uppercase letter-spaced headings** for modern elegance (Engel & Volkers)
167. **Editorial italic accents** — serif italic for luxury callouts (Essonnes Display style)
168. **Heading hierarchy**: H1 at 48-60px down to H6 at 16-18px, all bold (700 weight) (Engel & Volkers)
169. **Font-size accessibility controls** for vision-impaired users (select sites)

### Color Schemes
170. **"Quiet luxury" palette** — warm beiges, deep olives, rich charcoals (2026 trend)
171. **Dark navy/black backgrounds** with white typography for premium feel (Coldwell Banker, Christie's)
172. **Gold/champagne accent color** (#e0bc75) for CTAs and highlights (luxury pattern)
173. **Minimal accent color** — one signature brand color used sparingly (Compass: #0064E5 blue)
174. **High contrast** ratios for text legibility and WCAG compliance (all premium sites)
175. **Subtle gray tones** (#f4f4f4 backgrounds, #757575 secondary text) for layering (Engel & Volkers)

### White Space
176. **Generous section padding** — 50-80px between major sections (all premium sites)
177. **"Breathing room" philosophy** — intentional emptiness as design element, not wasted space (2026 trend)
178. **Narrow content containers** for readability (max-width: 658px for article content)
179. **Card spacing** — consistent gaps between property cards (15-40px)

### Animations & Interactions
180. **Scroll-triggered fade-in** — elements appear as they enter viewport (all premium sites)
181. **Scroll-triggered slide-up** — content slides up into position on scroll (Christie's, Sotheby's)
182. **Parallax depth effect** — background moves slower than foreground on scroll (The Agency, Corcoran)
183. **Smooth page transitions** — fade or slide between page loads (SPA-style navigation)
184. **Hover state color transitions** — 100-200ms ease timing on buttons and links (Compass)
185. **Image hover zoom** — subtle scale transform on property card images on hover (all sites)
186. **Button hover effects** — background fill change, border transitions, gold-to-transparent (luxury pattern)
187. **Underline hover animation** — link underlines slide in from left on hover (Engel & Volkers)
188. **Custom cursor effects** — cursor changes shape near interactive elements (emerging trend)
189. **Loading shimmer animations** — gradient sweep placeholder while content loads (Compass)
190. **Carousel auto-advance** with pause-on-hover (Coldwell Banker)
191. **Accordion expand/collapse** — smooth height transition for FAQ/details (Compass, Engel & Volkers)
192. **Modal/overlay transitions** — fade-in backdrop with slide-in content panel (all sites)
193. **Counter animations** — numbers count up when stats section enters viewport (Coldwell Banker)
194. **Sticky elements** — CTAs or agent cards that remain visible during scroll (Compass property pages)

### Video Usage
195. **Homepage video hero** — cinematic, aspirational, no sound by default (Coldwell Banker, The Agency)
196. **Agent introduction videos** — personality-driven content on profile pages (Elliman)
197. **Property video tours** — professional cinematography embedded in listings (Sotheby's)

### Premium Visual Effects

## 10. TECHNICAL / PERFORMANCE

### Mobile Experience
272. **Mobile-first responsive design** — layouts adapt at 6+ breakpoints (375px, 416px, 512px, 768px, 1023px, 1280px+) (Compass)
273. **Touch-friendly tap targets** — minimum 48px height for buttons and links (all sites)
274. **Swipe gestures** on carousels and galleries (all sites)
275. **Simplified mobile navigation** — hamburger expanding to full-screen overlay (all sites)
276. **Mobile-optimized forms** — large inputs, appropriate keyboard types (number, email, tel) (all sites)
277. **Reduced content density** on mobile — hidden complex elements, single-column layouts (Compass)
278. **App download promotion** — banner or CTA for native iOS/Android app (Compass, Sotheby's)

### Page Speed & Performance
279. **Lazy loading** of images and below-fold content (all premium sites)
280. **Image optimization** — responsive srcset, WebP/AVIF formats, blur-up placeholders (Compass)
281. **Content-visibility CSS** — browser skips rendering off-screen sections (Compass)
282. **Shimmer/skeleton loading states** — gray animated placeholders while content loads (Compass)
283. **CDN delivery** — Akamai, Cloudflare, or similar for global performance (Christie's uses Akamai)
284. **Code splitting** — JavaScript loaded per-route, not monolithic bundle (SPA architectures)
285. **Font display: swap** — text visible immediately, styled font loads async (Engel & Volkers, The Agency)

### SEO Features
286. **Semantic HTML** — proper heading hierarchy (H1-H6), article/section/nav elements (all sites)
287. **Schema.org structured data** — PropertyListing, RealEstateAgent, LocalBusiness markup (The Agency, Engel & Volkers)
288. **Dynamic meta tags** — unique title, description, OG image per listing/page (all sites)
289. **XML sitemap** with all listings, agents, neighborhoods indexed (all sites)
290. **Canonical URLs** to prevent duplicate content (all sites)
291. **Optimized URL structure** — /homes-for-sale/city/neighborhood/ pattern (Compass)
292. **Breadcrumb navigation** with schema markup (The Agency, Engel & Volkers)
293. **Location landing pages** optimized for "[city] luxury real estate" queries (Sotheby's, Compass)

### Accessibility
294. **WCAG compliance** — color contrast ratios meeting AA standards (all premium sites)
295. **Screen reader support** — .sr-only / .visually-hidden classes for descriptive text (Compass, Engel & Volkers)
296. **ARIA attributes** — proper roles, labels, live regions for dynamic content (Compass)
297. **Keyboard navigation** — full tab/enter/escape support for menus, modals, carousels (Compass)
298. **Focus visible states** — clear outline/shadow on focused elements (Compass: blue box-shadow)
299. **Skip navigation links** for keyboard users (select sites)

301. **Font-size adjustment widget** and text-to-speech option (select premium sites)

### Analytics & Consent
