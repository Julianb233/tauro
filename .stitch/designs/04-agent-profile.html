About Sarah

Philadelphia's Most Trusted Buyer's Agent

Sarah Kim has been transforming Philadelphia homeownership dreams into reality for over 12 years. A Center City resident herself, she brings an insider's perspective to every transaction — from navigating the competitive Rittenhouse market to uncovering hidden gems in emerging neighborhoods like Fishtown and Northern Liberties.

Before joining Tauro, Sarah served as a portfolio analyst at a Philadelphia real estate investment firm, giving her unmatched insight into property valuations, market timing, and investment potential. That financial acumen, combined with her warmth and tenacity, consistently delivers above-ask results for both buyers and sellers.

Sarah's philosophy is simple: every client deserves the same white-glove service she'd want for herself. That commitment has earned her the Tauro "Elite Agent" designation three years running.
