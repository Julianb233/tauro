About the Neighborhood

Philadelphia's Crown Jewel

Rittenhouse Square is Philadelphia's most coveted neighborhood — a blend of Victorian brownstones, modern high-rises, and award-winning restaurants all centered around the beloved 19th-century park. Named after astronomer David Rittenhouse, the Square has long attracted the city's most prominent residents.

The neighborhood stretches from Broad Street to the Schuylkill River, Chestnut to Pine Streets, forming a dense, walkable urban village. World-class dining on Sansom and Walnut Streets, boutique shopping on Rittenhouse Row, and cultural institutions like the Curtis Institute of Music make this one of America's most livable urban neighborhoods.

Real estate here ranges from intimate pied-à-terre condos to sweeping 5,000+ sq ft penthouse residences. Tauro's agents have sold more Rittenhouse properties than any other brokerage in Philadelphia — three years running.
